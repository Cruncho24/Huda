Overall visual direction

What feeling should Huda give when you open it?

A

Premium Dark

Deep navy/slate backgrounds, gold accents, emerald highlights. Like a high-end prayer app — serious, elegant, focused. Think Calm or Headspace but Islamic.

B

Clean Light — Elevated

Pure white base, refined emerald accents, soft shadows and blur effects. Modern, airy, professional. Like the best banking or productivity apps — trustworthy and polished.

C

Warm & Spiritual

Warm off-whites, rich emerald + amber tones, slightly textured. Feels handcrafted and intimate — like a beautifully bound Quran or an illuminated manuscript brought into the digital age.
